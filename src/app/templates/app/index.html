Running for life, health and opportunity

What started out as a simple fundraiser has now become an annual spectacle with runners coming from all over the country to run the Chiltern Chase. We are so grateful to be able to support local organisations that so desperately need the funds.
If you would like to make a difference and help fund worthwhile charities then why not join us on our beautiful run. Race details above.

Our Lead Sponsors

Grundon

www.grundon.com

Grundon is one of the UK’s leading suppliers of integrated waste management and environmental solutions. Situated near to Ewelme Grundon has been a loyal sponsor to the Chiltern Chase.

Rowse Honey

www.rowsehoney.co.uk

Rowse started in 1938 when a chap called Tony Rowse started beekeeping at his home in Ewelme, Oxfordshire. He enjoyed it so much he decided to try and make a living from his hobby.

Fawley Farms

https://www.fawleyfarms.co.uk/

Fawley Farms is one of the largest producers of outdoor bred pigs in the UK. Fawley Farms is a well-established pig breeding company operating ten breeding herds on sites in Devon, Dorset, Hampshire, Oxfordshire and Wiltshire.

Hazel & Jefferies

www.hazellj.co.uk

Since 1971, Hazell and Jefferies Ltd. have been providing superior work and service when it comes to resurfacing a road.
